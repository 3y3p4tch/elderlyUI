Check

Posted Oct 23 2020-10-23T00:00:00+05:30 by Team
Updated Dec 16 2020-12-16T10:32:37+05:30

Checklist for the product after considering the cause, context, and comprehension of the problem:

  1. Minimal Interface with only essential apps for easy navigating
  2. Easier access to settings for changing language
  3. Options to increase the font size (like A A+ A++ on notification bar)
  4. The keypad in native language with auto-complete/word completion
  5. User-Friendly Design
  6. Quick Search Option on Homescreen
